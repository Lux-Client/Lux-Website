Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1 Responsible Body

If you have any questions regarding data protection, please contact the MCLC developer team via our support Discord.

2 Data Collection in the Launcher

The MCLC Launcher is designed to collect as little data as possible. Local data such as profiles, settings, and logs remain on your device.

3 Authentication

When you log in with your Microsoft account, we use OAuth2. MCLC never stores your password. Access tokens are stored locally encrypted on your computer.

4 Web Analytics

Our website may use anonymized analytics to understand site usage. Details can be found in our Opt-Out document.

5 Your Rights

You have the right to information, correction, or deletion of your data, as far as it has been stored by us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you can contact us via our Discord Support Server.
